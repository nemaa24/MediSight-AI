# MediSight AI
### AI Transparency Label for Healthcare Prediction Systems

## About
MediSight AI is a clinical decision support system that predicts 
disease risk and generates a standardized AI Transparency Label 
for every prediction — covering accuracy, explainability, 
fairness, and ethical compliance.

## Diseases Covered
- Diabetes
- Heart Disease  
- Liver Disease
- Chronic Kidney Disease
